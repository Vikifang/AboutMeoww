Artist Name: BONG SIAW FANG
Title: “About Still-Life”
Medium: Charcoal and Soft Pastel
Size: 42 x 59.4 cm (A2 Size)
Year: 2023
Artist's Statement

This Still-Life show a human is sitting
in the middle with around of objects.
The human present an arrogant attitude
,oblivious to the things around you.
Fruits symbolise harvest, pleasures ,
overindulgence and dissonance.
I choose this picture because of the composition
that can show the space between
each subject and the contrast lighting to show the subject.
